## Summary

History often depends on small events that appear minor at the time. A decision, an encounter, or a few seconds of timing may later become the turning point of a much larger story. The skiing memory in the lesson shows how excitement can hide danger, especially when weather and confidence make risk harder to see. The deeper lesson is humility: life can change quickly when risk, choice, and chance meet.

## Grammar Focus

1. Contrast with but  
   The snow looked exciting, but the conditions were unstable.

2. Cause-effect verbs  
   A small decision can lead to a major outcome.

3. Abstract noun theme  
   The deeper point is humility.

## Important Sentences

- History often turns on small events.
- A small avalanche first seemed like an adventure, but it exposed how much risk was hiding under the surface.
- Life can hang by a thread when risk, choice, and chance meet.
