# Why Europe Wants to Tear Down Thousands of Its Dams

Level: B2  
Topic: Environment and River Restoration  
Suggested WordPress visibility: Password protected if you include classroom-only handout text or extended article notes.

## 1. Lesson Overview

本單元搭配 National Geographic 文章〈Why Europe wants to tear down thousands of its dams〉與閱讀講義，訓練學測閱讀所需的標題預測、文章架構、上下文猜字、題型定位與摘要能力。

Source: Rebecca Dzombak, National Geographic, published May 21, 2026.  
Article URL: https://www.nationalgeographic.com/environment/article/europe-dam-removal-rivers-hydropower

## 2. Learning Goals

- 能用標題預測文章主題：dam removal、river health、ecosystem restoration。
- 能抓出文章架構：問題、人物例子、政策背景、生態原因、反方疑慮、結論。
- 能練習學測常見題型：主旨、細節、字義、推論與作者態度。
- 能用 5-6 句英文摘要文章，而不是逐句翻譯。

## 3. Pre-Reading

看到標題 Why Europe Wants to Tear Down Thousands of Its Dams，先不要急著查單字。先預測：

- 歐洲為什麼要拆水壩？
- 老舊水壩可能造成什麼問題？
- 作者可能支持還是反對 dam removal？
- 你預期會看到哪些字：dam, river, barrier, restore, ecosystem, species, hydropower?

## 4. Article Structure

| Function | Efficient Summary | Test Focus |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Introduction | Europe has a growing movement to remove old and unnecessary dams. | Main idea / title |
| Example | A Norwegian fisherman helped remove a defunct dam. | Detail |
| Policy | The E.U. set a target for free-flowing rivers by 2030. | Purpose / detail |
| Ecological reasons | Dams can block migration, change river flow, and create stagnant water. | Cause-effect |
| Development | Dam removals have gained momentum in recent years. | Chronological order |
| Opposition | People may worry about flooding, cost, and local change. | Inference / attitude |
| Balance | Europe must balance river health with hydropower needs. | Author attitude |

## 7. Reading Signals

| Signal | Function | Reading Meaning |
| --- | --- | --- |
| because / driven by / to | reason or purpose | remove dams to restore rivers |
| but / however | contrast | dam removal helps nature, but some people oppose it |
| as / while | simultaneous development | pressure strengthens as climate changes |
| if | condition | if a project harms daily life, opposition may grow |
| once | time condition | once removal is done, nature can recover |

## 8. GSAT-Style Practice

1. What is the main idea of the article?  
   A. Europe is building more dams for hydropower.  
   B. Europe is removing old dams to restore river ecosystems.  
   C. Norway is banning all fishing activities.  
   D. Old dams are becoming tourist attractions.

2. Why can a defunct dam be a good candidate for removal?  
   A. It is new and powerful.  
   B. It has been inactive and no longer useful.  
   C. It is a natural waterfall.  
   D. It always prevents flooding.

3. Which problem caused by dams is mentioned?  
   A. They make rivers too clean.  
   B. They stop all climate change.  
   C. They block animals from moving along rivers.  
   D. They make fish migrate too quickly.

4. The word defunct is closest in meaning to ________.  
   A. no longer active  
   B. newly built  
   C. highly dangerous  
   D. naturally beautiful

5. What can be inferred from local opposition to dam removal?  
   A. People may fear change even when nature can benefit.  
   B. All people in Europe oppose dam removal.  
   C. Dams never affect ecosystems.  
   D. Removing dams is always free.

6. The author's attitude toward dam removal is best described as ________.  
   A. supportive but aware of challenges  
   B. completely negative  
   C. uninterested  
   D. humorous and careless

## 10. Reflection Paragraph

我以 National Geographic 的環境議題文章作為學測閱讀訓練素材。練習時，我先從標題預測主題，再用段落功能整理文章架構，抓出問題、例子、政策、原因、反方疑慮與結論。接著，我依學測題型練習主旨題、細節題、字義題、推論題與作者態度題，並把 defunct、barrier、restore、ecosystem、stagnant、controversial 等單字放回文章情境中記憶。透過這種方式，我能建立高效率閱讀流程，提升理解長篇英文文章與判斷選項的能力。
