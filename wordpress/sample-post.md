# What Never Changes in a Changing World

Level: B2

Estimated Time: 12 minutes

## 1. Lesson Overview

這是一篇短篇自建示範教材，幫助學習者練習從歷史與人類行為中找出不變的模式。

## 2. Learning Goals

- 理解短文中關於歷史與人類行為的主要觀點。
- 學會使用 behaviour、preview、timeless 等 B2 單字。
- 練習用英文說明一個不容易改變的人類行為。

## 3. Original Text

The world changes faster than any classroom can explain. New tools appear, old plans break, and a single event can cripple a carefully built system. Still, some patterns stay surprisingly steady.

People still want safety, status, meaning, and connection. These needs shape markets, families, classrooms, and even geopolitical decisions. A new dialect of technology may appear, but the human questions underneath remain relevant.

But the behaviours that never change are history's most powerful lessons. Because they preview what to expect in the future.

## 4. Vocabulary

| Word | IPA | Part of Speech | 中文 | English Definition | Example |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| behaviour | /bɪˈheɪvjər/ | noun | 行為；表現 | the way a person or group acts | Human behaviour often follows familiar patterns. |
| preview | /ˈpriːvjuː/ | verb | 預告；預先顯示 | to show what something may be like before it happens | Past choices can preview future risks. |
| timeless | /ˈtaɪmləs/ | adjective | 永恆的；不受時間影響的 | not affected by changes in time or fashion | Kindness is a timeless value. |

## 5. Phrases

- **what to expect**: 可以預期的事情。
- **remain relevant**: 仍然有關、仍然重要。

## 6. Grammar

### Relative clause with that

The clause "that never change" describes "behaviours".

## 7. Important Sentences

1. But the behaviours that never change are history's most powerful lessons.
2. Because they preview what to expect in the future.
